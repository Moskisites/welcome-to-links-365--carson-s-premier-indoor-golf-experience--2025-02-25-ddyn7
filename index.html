About Links-365

Founded in 2023 by a team of passionate golf professionals and tech enthusiasts, Links-365 emerged from a vision to make premium golf accessible year-round in Carson City. Our facility combines cutting-edge technology with traditional golf expertise to create an unmatched indoor golfing experience.

Our mission is to help every golfer reach their full potential through technology, expert instruction, and community. We believe in creating an inclusive environment where players of all skill levels can improve their game, connect with fellow enthusiasts, and experience the joy of golf regardless of weather conditions.
